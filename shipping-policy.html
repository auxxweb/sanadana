1. Shipping Areas


Sanadana Herbals ships to most locations across India. We use reliable courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders.


2. Processing Time


Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During peak seasons or special promotions, processing may take up to 3-4 business days.


3. Shipping Methods


We offer various shipping options:


  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (additional charges apply)
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select cities (subject to availability)

4. Shipping Charges


Shipping charges are calculated based on:


  • Product weight and dimensions
  • Delivery location
  • Selected shipping method

Free shipping may be available on orders above a certain value. Check our website for current offers.


5. Order Tracking


Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can track your order status using the provided tracking number on the courier's website.


6. Delivery Issues


If you experience any delivery issues:


  • Contact us immediately at sanadanaherbals@gmail.com or +91 9497 577 660
  • Provide your order number and tracking details
  • Our team will investigate and resolve the issue promptly
