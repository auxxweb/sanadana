    Yes. You can easily place orders through WhatsApp with guidance from our support team.

  • Do you deliver across India?

    Yes. We offer delivery to most locations across India through reliable courier partners.

  • Are there any side effects?

    Our ayurvedic products are made with natural ingredients. People with existing medical conditions should consult a healthcare professional before use.

  • What is herbal cough syrup and how does it work?

    Herbal cough syrup is prepared using ayurvedic herbs that support respiratory health naturally by soothing the throat and balancing the body without harsh chemicals.

    Ayurvedic medicine for piles focuses on digestion, vein health, and lifestyle balance, aiming to address root causes naturally over time.

  • Are your products suitable for long-term use?

    Many ayurvedic and herbal products are traditionally used for long-term wellness when taken as directed and aligned with a healthy lifestyle.

  • Are Sanadana Herbals products GMP certified?

    Yes. All Sanadana Herbals products are manufactured in GMP-certified facilities to ensure quality, purity, and safety.

  • Can herbal products be used alongside modern medicine?

    Ayurvedic and herbal products are often used as supportive wellness solutions. If you are on medication, it is best to consult a healthcare professional.
